rocky-road-fudge

Description

Learn how to make a delicious rocky road fudge from home! This fudge includes creamy milk chocolate, marshmallows and almonds!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup of chopped almonds

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, pour can of sweetened condensed milk and 2 cups of chocolate chips
  2. Microwave mixing bowl with ingredients for 90 seconds while taking bowl out halfway and mixing and continue microwaving
  3. Once done, add the 1 tsp of vanilla extract and mix
  4. While waiting for mixing bowl to cool, start chopping up 1 cup of almonds
  5. Finally, pour the 2 cups of marshmallows and almonds into mixing bowl give it one final mix.
  6. In a 7x7 tray, pour in mixture. Store in fridge for 2 hours before cutting.
