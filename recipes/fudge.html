rocky-road-fudge

Description

Learn how to make a delicious rocky road fudge from home! This fudge includes creamy milk chocolate, marshmallows and almonds!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup of chopped almonds
